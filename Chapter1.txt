As the sun set behind the mountains, the world was bathed in a warm, golden glow. The air was still, and the only sound was the gentle rustling of the leaves in the trees. In the distance, a lone wolf howled, its mournful cry echoing across the valley. And for a moment, everything seemed to stand still, as if the world was holding its breath in anticipation of what would come next. But then, slowly and surely, the darkness began to creep in, shrouding the land in shadow and bringing with it a sense of mystery and magic that could only be found under the cover of night.
